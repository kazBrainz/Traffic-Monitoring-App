What is CompTIA Security+?

CompTIA Security+ is a globally recognized certification that validates the baseline skills necessary to perform core security functions and pursue an IT security career.

Benefits of Security+

  • Prove your competency in cybersecurity
  • Gain a competitive edge in the job market
  • Meet DoD 8570 compliance requirements
  • Prepare for advanced security certifications
